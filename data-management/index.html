What is the.medicines.network?

the.medicines.network is a platform to allow access to up-to-date and accurate medicinal products information using state-of-the-art decentralised technologies like blockchain and web3.

What is mi-manager?

mi-manager offers a simple and intuitive way to manage your content on the.medicines.network. Using mi-manager you can add, update or remove content, and control who can perform these functions.

Support the community?

Make your vote count. As a content provider to the.medicines.network you can control at a granular level and influence who has access to your public-facing data.

Own your data. Get insights

You are the owner of your data. You should know who has access, what they're doing it and when they're doing it. Insights come as standard on the.medicines.network as part of a contractual obligation.
